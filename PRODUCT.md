# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High-end automotive enthusiasts, potential BMW customers, and design connoisseurs who appreciate premium digital experiences and high-performance engineering. They are likely visiting to explore the aesthetic and technical prowess of the BMW M-Series in a "museum-grade" digital environment.

## Product Purpose
A high-impact, premium showcase website for BMW car models (starting with the M4 GT3). It aims to demonstrate the intersection of aggressive sport performance and sophisticated engineering through cinematic visuals, bold typography, and technical precision.

## Brand Personality
Aggressive, Precise, Sophisticated.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages with purple/blue neon glows.
- Cluttered, information-heavy dealership sites.
- "Inter" font or generic Bootstrap-style 3-column grids.
- Weak, low-contrast "light gray for elegance" typography.

## Design Principles
- **Aggressive Sport**: Use high-contrast light/dark sections and bold, track-tight typography to convey power.
- **Museum-Grade Curation**: Every element should feel intentionally placed, with massive whitespace and "document-style" precision.
- **Kinetic Tension**: Motion should feel heavy and purposeful, simulating physical mass and spring physics.
- **Tactile Detail**: Use "Double-Bezel" nested architecture for containers to simulate high-end hardware.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- Strict adherence to `prefers-reduced-motion` for all cinematic transitions.
- High-contrast text ratios (≥4.5:1).
